The Art of Writing Clean Code

Writing clean, effective and maintainable code is essential for any long-living software product. By neglecting that fact, teams bring up a time bomb - step by step, imperfection creeps into the code, infecting different parts of the system until system is put on its knees. Continuously keeping internal structure of a product in a good shape is a big challenge for the team - it takes years to master a ream of substantial techniques, practices and patterns… But the project has already begun. In this training, in a short time you’ll find out how to write code that you, your colleagues and followers will adore. This, in turn, will make your products and customers shine.

Practical Domain-Driven Design

Domain model is in the heart of every enterprise application. Nowadays developers spend too much time working on "surrondings", tackling deeply technical, challenging issues. However, it's a core domain which makes business spin, while everything around has just a supporting function. Domain-Driven Design or DDD is an excellent way to approach complex software problems, and make them far more understandable and manageable. Unfortunately, developers find DDD pretty hard to grasp because of poorly-written materials available online, lack of good examples and industry-spread experience. Because of this, vitally important practice is left aboard. In fact, DDD is pretty simple if attacked from the right perspective. Learn how to do both tactical and strategical DDD right.

How it's gonna look like?

How long it's gonna take?

2 days.

Specification By Example

Specification by example is a collaborative approach to defining requirements as executable examples instead of abstract statements. It is applied in the context of agile software development methods. This approach is particularly successful for managing requirements and functional tests on large-scale projects of significant domain and organisational complexity. The workshop is based on Gojko Adzic's books "Bridging the Communication Gap" and "Specification by Example", as well as our personal experience developing, leading and testing software development projects using this approach.

How it's gonna look like?

We provide interactive workshop aimed to tell You about tools and practices that can help adopt You Specification by example practice in Your daily job and solve communication gap between all parties involved in software development process.

How long it's gonna take?

2 days.

Am I eligible?

Yes if you're developer / QA engineer / team leader / business analyst willing to improve comminucation inside Your software development project.

Software Architecture for Developers

In the age of agile software development and cross-functional teams, everyone is an architect. Software architecture is not about big up-front design with UMLs and circles on the whiteboard - it's about practices and techniques you have to follow in order to keep design clean under a rain of changing requirements, things that are extremely important for fighting design entropy. As a professional developer you should know how to cope with evolutionary design and avoid "code & fix" insanity. Spread your knowlege beyond coding - learn how to lead the way and do "just enough" software architecture by youself. Define, ensure and articulate software architeture in your team, maintain technical vision and keep balance with agility.

How it's gonna look like?

Together we'll dive deeply into essential software architecture techniques. You'll discover modern architectural patterns, paradigms and tools, understand why there is no conflict between agile and architecture. Optionally you will be sent a practical homework assignment with detailed solution feedback upon completion.

2 days.
